Human-Web

Tiny stick figures that wander around your website. Add with one script tag.

What is this?

Human-Web adds playful 3D stick figure characters to any website. They walk around, react to your mouse, and bring life to your pages.

How to use

Just add one line to your HTML: <script src="human-web.js"></script>
